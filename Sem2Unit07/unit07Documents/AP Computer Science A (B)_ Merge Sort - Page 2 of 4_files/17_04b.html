Part 2

The only way to really understand the Merge sort is to see it in action and to play with it until it sinks in! This is one of those algorithms that starts to make perfect sense, but the code can look a little daunting. You may find that you need to take a break and come back to the Merge sort several times.

  • Download the Virtual Lecture Notes to the Unit07 Documents folder and print a copy for your notebook.
  • It is worth repeating that the Merge sort is challenging. Go through the Lecture Notes one step at a time and you will understand it more clearly.
  • Download the TestListing5.txt file to your Unit07 Lessons Folder.
  • Change the file's extension from TXT to JAVA.

As with the Selection and Insertion sort, your index cards are invaluable when it comes to understanding the Merge sort. Parents and younger children are particularly impressed with the Merge sort, so show off your skills.
